Coastal Change Analytics for Victoria

CoastWatch Victoria translates complex coastal monitoring data into actionable intelligence for decision-makers, researchers, and communities. By integrating UAV photogrammetry with wave monitoring and advanced analytics, this platform reveals how Victoria's beaches are changing, why these changes occur, and what this means for coastal management and climate adaptation.

Why This Platform Matters

Data-Driven Coastal Management: Traditional coastal monitoring provides snapshots. CoastWatch Victoria delivers the full story by tracking beach evolution over years, correlating changes with wave forcing, and identifying vulnerability patterns that inform proactive management strategies.

Accessible Science: We transform technical datasets into intuitive visualizations and metrics that bridge the gap between scientific research and practical decision-making. Whether you're a coastal manager assessing erosion risk, a researcher studying climate impacts, or a community member concerned about your local beach, this platform provides the insights you need.

Scalable Framework: Built on the Victorian Coastal Monitoring Program infrastructure, CoastWatch Victoria demonstrates how systematic monitoring and analytics can be deployed statewide, creating a comprehensive picture of coastal change across diverse settings from embayed beaches to open coastlines.

Our Methodology

Our integrated approach combines UAV surveys with wave monitoring to understand the relationship between ocean forcing and coastal change
