Coastal Change Analytics for Victoria

CoastWatch Victoria translates complex coastal monitoring data into actionable intelligence for decision-makers, researchers, and communities. By integrating UAV photogrammetry with wave monitoring and advanced analytics, we reveal how Victoria's beaches are changing, why these changes occur, and what this means for coastal management and climate adaptation.

Why This Platform Matters

Data-Driven Coastal Management: Traditional coastal monitoring provides snapshots. CoastWatch Victoria delivers the full story—tracking beach evolution over years, correlating changes with wave forcing, and identifying vulnerability patterns that inform proactive management strategies.

Accessible Science: We transform technical datasets into intuitive visualizations and metrics that bridge the gap between scientific research and practical decision-making. Whether you're a coastal manager assessing erosion risk, a researcher studying climate impacts, or a community member concerned about your local beach, this platform provides the insights you need.

Scalable Framework: Built on the Victorian Coastal Monitoring Program infrastructure, CoastWatch Victoria demonstrates how systematic monitoring and analytics can be deployed statewide, creating a comprehensive picture of coastal change across diverse settings from embayed beaches to open coastlines.

The Victorian Coastal Monitoring Program

The Victorian Coastal Monitoring Program (VCMP) was established in 2017 with three core objectives:

  • Increase our understanding of how coastlines change over time
  • Assist with managing coasts in the present
  • Provide knowledge to ensure coasts are resilient in the future

Increasing sea levels and more frequent storm events mean that Victoria's coastline may be subjected to increasing coastal erosion and inundation. Coastal monitoring is crucial to understanding coastal change and provides an evidentiary basis for sound coastal management and adaptation.

A critical goal of the VCMP is to aggregate and integrate data from the local to statewide scale, to aid decision-making at local and regional levels. The VCMP is now a major field monitoring and knowledge management program that provides coastal managers and communities with information on coastal processes and hazards.
